AI-Powered Workplace Productivity Assistant
Project Overview

The AI-Powered Workplace Productivity Assistant is a practical AI solution developed as part of the CAPACITI AI Skills Accelerator Programme. The project aims to improve workplace efficiency by automating repetitive tasks such as email writing, meeting note summarization, task planning, and research assistance.

The solution demonstrates the effective use of Artificial Intelligence, prompt engineering, and responsible AI practices to solve real-world business challenges. By reducing manual effort and improving productivity, the assistant helps professionals focus on higher-value activities.

Features
1. Smart Email Generator
Generates professional emails based on user requirements.
Supports multiple tones such as formal, informal, and persuasive.
Adapts content for different audiences including managers, clients, and team members.
Creates clear subject lines and professional formatting.
2. Meeting Notes Summarizer
Converts lengthy meeting notes into concise summaries.
Extracts key discussion points and decisions.
Identifies action items, deadlines, and responsible individuals.
Improves information sharing and follow-up tracking.
3. AI Task Planner
Creates structured daily and weekly schedules.
Prioritizes tasks based on urgency and importance.
Recommends time-management strategies.
Helps users improve productivity and organization.
4. AI Research Assistant
Summarizes articles, reports, and workplace documents.
Highlights key insights and recommendations.
Simplifies complex information for quick understanding.
Supports faster decision-making and knowledge acquisition.
